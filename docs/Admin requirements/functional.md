# Admin Functional Requirements
## GoShopMe Admin Application

## 11. Reporting & Analytics

### 11.1 Subscription Reports

Admins can view:

Active subscriptions by plan (weekly / monthly / annual)

Subscription growth over time

Churn and cancellations

Upgrade and downgrade activity

### 11.2 Free Trial Reports

Admins can view:

Number of active free trials

Trial start and end dates

Trial-to-paid conversion rate

Drop-off before trial completion

### 11.3 Active User Reports

Admins can view:

Daily active users (DAU)

Weekly active users (WAU)

Monthly active users (MAU)

Active users by country and region

### 11.4 Revenue Reports

Admins can view revenue split by:

Subscriptions

Marketplace commissions

Affiliate commissions

Total revenue over time

Revenue by country and currency

### 11.5 Onboarding Channel Attribution

Admins can view user acquisition sources:

Facebook

Instagram

TikTok

Organic search

Direct

Sharing / invite links

Campaign-specific sources

Reports include:

User count per channel

Conversion to subscription per channel

Revenue per channel

### 11.6 Custom Filters & Export

Admins can:

Filter reports by date range, country, channel

Export reports (CSV / future BI tools)
