# Personas (MVP-Focused)

This document defines the core personas relevant to the MVP of GoShopMe. Personas are intentionally narrow and tied only to the MVP features, avoiding post-MVP user types.

## Persona 1 — The Busy Professional Shopper (Primary MVP User)

**Name:** Emma, 32

### Profile:
Emma works in a corporate environment, has disposable income, wants to look polished, but has no time to shop.

### Goals:
• Find outfits fast without scrolling endlessly
• Look stylish for work, social events, and dating
• Get help choosing the right size and style
• Recreate outfits she sees on Instagram
• Save money with smart alternatives
• Share products with friends and earn rewards

### Frustrations:
• Too much choice → decision fatigue
• Doesn't know if items match together
• Influencer items are often expensive or sold out
• Hard to find similar cheaper versions
• Shopping on different websites feels fragmented

### MVP Needs:
• AI chat that understands natural language
• Style quiz to personalize suggestions
• Upload photo/video → get outfit reconstruction
• Smart alternatives (luxury vs. cheaper)
• Conversational checkout
• Earn commission when friends buy
• Save favorite items and revisit later

## Persona 2 — The Trend-Driven Social Shopper (MVP Viral Growth Persona)

**Name:** Sofia, 26

### Profile:
Highly active on TikTok/Instagram, loves discovering and sharing looks. Frequently buys for going out, travel, and trends.

### Goals:
• Stay ahead of trends
• Easily share outfits with friends
• Try luxury-inspired looks without the price
• Build confidence with outfits that "fit the vibe"
• Shop together with friends → group chat vibes

### Frustrations:
• Hard to find exact TikTok/Instagram outfits
• Shopping links are often broken or unavailable
• Wants a friend's validation before buying
• Gets overwhelmed by too many store choices

### MVP Needs:
• Upload look → find exact / similar pieces
• Social sharing → invite friends to the app
• Group chat with AI + friends
• Outfit suggestions for occasions (party, holiday, date night)
• Commission model where she earns if friends buy her shared items

## Persona 3 — The Friend Invited to Shop (Secondary MVP Persona)

**Name:** Lily, 28

### Profile:
Does not actively look for styling apps but joins because a friend shared a product.

### Goals:
• Quickly see what her friend shared
• Add her own input (e.g., "I like the black option better")
• Discover recommended items without too much complexity
• Feel part of a collaborative shopping moment

### Frustrations:
• Doesn't want to install unnecessary apps
• Needs fast value → must immediately see the benefit
• Hates complicated onboarding

### MVP Needs:
• Seamless invitation flow
• Immediate access to shared items
• Lightweight onboarding
• Option to continue the conversation in the shared AI chat

## Persona 4 — Internal Stylist / Curator (Internal Only)

**Name:** Alex, 29

### Profile:
A hired stylist who curates "Creator Picks" collections for the MVP (since external creators are not part of the MVP yet).

### Goals:
• Create high-quality outfit collections
• Match styles to fashion trends
• Produce consistent aesthetic for the home page
• Ensure curated items work well with the AI recommendations

### Frustrations:
• Needs easy way to upload collections
• Requires consistency across product images, tags, categories

### MVP Needs:
• Basic back-office interface for uploading curated looks
• Ability to define: item sets, occasions, themes
• Preview how collections appear to users

## Persona 5 — GoShopMe Admin (Internal MVP Role)

**Name:** Anna, 35

### Profile:
Oversees catalog updates, commissions, demo merchant data, and customer issues.

### Goals:
• Manage demo merchant catalog
• Review commissions for accuracy
• Monitor product availability
• Validate styling collections

### Frustrations:
• Manual data entry without proper tooling
• Need for simple validation workflows

### MVP Needs:
• Basic admin dashboard (React or simple web UI)
• CRUD access to:
  o Products
  o Users
  o Orders
  o Commission logs
  o Stats/analytics overview

## Summary of MVP Personas

| Persona | Included in MVP? | Primary Role |
|---------|------------------|--------------|
| Busy Professional Shopper | Yes | Core user of AI shopping + checkout |
| Trend-Driven Social Shopper | Yes | Drives virality and sharing |
| Invited Friend | Yes | Converts social traffic into users |
| Internal Stylist | Yes | Creates curated collections |
| Admin | Yes | Manages catalog, earnings, and content |
